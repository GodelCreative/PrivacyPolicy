Collection and Processing of Data

Use of the App:
Our app can generally be used without providing personal data. No user accounts are created.

The app stores certain data locally on your device, such as high scores and settings. This data is stored exclusively on your device and is not shared with third parties.

AdMob is used to display advertisements. In this context, Google and its partners may process the following data:

This data may be used to personalize advertising.

Further information:
https://policies.google.com/privacy


Legal Basis for Processing:
Processing is carried out in accordance with Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest), in particular for financing and improving the app through advertising.

If consent is obtained, processing is carried out in accordance with Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR.


Data Sharing:
We do not directly share your data. However, data may be processed by third-party services (e.g. Google AdMob).


Your Rights:
You have the right to access your stored data, rectification, deletion, restriction of processing, objection to processing, and data portability.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.


Data Retention:
We do not store personal data ourselves. Locally stored data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or delete the data within the app.
